## Notes on Copy Style

- **Tone:** Direct, technical but accessible, confident without being boastful
- **Structure:** Headline → Subheadline → Value Prop → Services → CTA
- **Length:** Keep paragraphs to 1-2 sentences max
- **Focus:** Outcomes and capabilities, not just features
- **Consistency:** Each landing page follows the same structure for easy navigation
